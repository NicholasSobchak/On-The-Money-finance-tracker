Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Information We Collect

On The Money collects only the information you explicitly provide:

  • Account credentials: Username and password for authentication
  • Financial data: Bank account information and transactions retrieved through Plaid, only when you explicitly link an account
  • Manual entries: Financial data you manually enter into the application

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personal financial tracking services
  • To authenticate your identity and protect your account
  • To sync your bank data through Plaid's secure API

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored on self-hosted infrastructure
  • Financial data is encrypted at rest using PostgreSQL pgcrypto
  • All communications use TLS 1.2+ encryption
  • Multi-factor authentication is available for account protection

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Plaid: When you explicitly link a bank account, your data is transmitted through Plaid's secure API to retrieve account information. Plaid's own privacy policy applies to their handling of your data.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your data at any time through the application's settings.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data and account
  • Export your data in CSV format
